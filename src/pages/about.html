About

instant (pl. -s) — (1) An infinitesimal space of time, esp. a point in time separating two states (at the instant of death); (2) The present or current month.

This is an online chat site that focuses on the very moment. It does (in general) not store the messages posted — unless someone takes care, they're gone as soon as the last participant closes the tab.

Have fun!
